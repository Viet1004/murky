Sync masks across devices
Off by default. Saves your picked sites and focus prompt to your account so they reappear on other browsers.
When sync is on, your account is the source of truth: edits on this device push up to your account immediately, and changes from other devices pull down on startup. Murky uploads to your account: 
user_site_selectors:
  origin            "https://www.youtube.com"
  card_selector     ".ytm-shorts-lockup-view-model-v2"
  href/image/title/price_selector  (optional)
  label             "YouTube Shorts"
  saved_at, updated_at

user_preferences:
  focus_prompt      "minimalist kitchen tools"
  scorer_id         "heuristic"
  updated_at
We do not send page contents, your browsing history, or anything from sites you haven't picked. Turn off any time — uploads stop immediately. Use "Delete sync data" below to also wipe what's on the server.

Powerful online filter — coming soon
Off by default. Sends each scored product to a server-side model that's larger than what runs locally. Only useful for harder filtering tasks (semantic / cross-language).
When the online filter is on, each product Murky scores sends: 
scored_product:
  origin            "https://shopee.vn"
  product_title     "Cotton T-shirt White M"
  product_price_vnd 199000          (when available)
  image_url         (the product image URL)
  focus_prompt      (your current focus prompt)
  scored_at         (timestamp)
We do not send: page contents outside what you mask, browsing history, other tabs, or your account email. Off by default. When you turn it off, no further uploads happen. Use "Delete filter data" to wipe what's already on the server.

Collect behavioral data
Off by default. Sends impressions, clicks, and (optional) regret responses to train the impulsive-mode detector.
When behavioral collection is on, Murky sends batched events: 
events:
  type              "impression" | "unmask" | "remask" | "click" | "page_view"
  product_key       "shopee:12345"
  was_masked        true|false
  latency_ms        time to first interaction
  page              {url, page_type, query, category_path}
  client_ts         timestamp

regret responses (only after a gentle prompt):
  trace_id, product_key, response, dwell_ms
We do not link this to your account email or to your Murky-server identity beyond an anonymous install id. Wipe anytime with the button below.
